Technical Experience

Book Finder

I built a web app that stores and suggests books from a book list. Users can recommend books they have enjoyed, and to also enter characteristics of a book that they would like to read. It matches their preferences with books stored in a realtime database and displays the top results.

Autocomplete Project

I developed an algorithm that efficiently sorts search terms that begin with a given query. It functions similarly to the suggested terms list generated in Google searches. The algorithms sorts terms based first on their likelihood to occur, and then alphabetically.

Work Experience

Brooklyn Defender Services

I used a program that compares lists of current and former inmates to update the firm’s client directory, as well as it's catalogue of clients eligible for parole. I imaged over 20 new computers and assisted in updating computer systems in order to achieve optimal performance.

Gruss Lipper Biophotonics Lab

I developed and tested a near-infrared biosensor. This biosensor is a chemical tool that detects the activity of RhoA, a protein whose functionality is important for many different types of cancer. Unlike previous biosensors, this version allows for the visualization of tumors and cells in living organisms. Ultimately, I developed two versions: one with better efficiency, and another with higher protein expression.

Casanova Lab

I genetically analyzed DNA samples in order to experimentally determine whether individuals are genetically susceptible to acquire tuberculosis. In order to do this, I amplified DNA and then a specific part of the sequence through software to analyze it. After reading this sequence, I identified the genotype of each individual and determined whether they were genetically susceptible to TB.

Uruguayan Consulate

I helped with the consulate’s outreach by researching Uruguayan events in New York and identifying organizations that might be interested in collaborating with the consulate to host events. I also located universities with a Latin American Studies program that recent immigrants might be interested in studying. Overall, I increased the consulate’s outreach program by 20%.
